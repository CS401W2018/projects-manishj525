Welcome to Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a beautiful white marble tomb in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It symbolizes love and houses her tomb.

The building's design, combining elements of several cultures, includes a large dome, gardens, minarets, and elaborate semi-precious stone decorations. Throughout the day, the building's hue varies according to the light.
