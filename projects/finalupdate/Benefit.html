Benefits of Staying Fit

Fitness improves both physical and mental health. Here are some benefits:

  • Strength

  • The ability of your muscles to lift weights or resist force is known as strength. It improves general physical performance and everyday activities.

  • Bodyweight

  • Exercises:Exercises that use your body weight as support to increase strength include push-ups, squats, and planks. They can be performed anywhere and don't require any special equipment.

  • Weightlifting

  • Lifting weights, barbells, or other weights to increase strength and muscle is known as weightlifting. It works well for focusing on particular muscle groups.
