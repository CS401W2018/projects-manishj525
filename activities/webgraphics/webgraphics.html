What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

JPEG is great for detailed photos like landscapes or portraits with many colors. It compresses well but loses some quality.

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

GIF works well for simple graphics, animations, or images with few colors. It supports transparency and animation.

What is unique about the PNG format?

PNG keeps all image details without losing quality and supports transparent backgrounds. It's perfect for logos or images needing sharp edges.

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

Use photo editing software like Photoshop or free online tools (e.g., Canva) to adjust the size before uploading it to your site.
