1.The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

The browser knows where to send the form data by the action property in a form tag.

2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

How the data is sent is specified by the method property, which can be either "POST" or "GET."

3. GET sends data visibly in the URL and is used for simple requests.

POST is used for safe data transfer and is invisible. GET is used for basic requests and sends data that is visible in the URL.
